Governmental Tingz

Type of Government

Representative Democracy
A type of government where a group of elected officials will represent the people of the country. Different from direct democracy, as a direct democracy involves individuals to directly vote for certain policies.

This form of government was chosen as it is flexible, and ensures that people do have a say in how their country works by choosing a group to represent them.
